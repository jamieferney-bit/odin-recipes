Carbonara

A simple italian pasta dish that you can make with minimal ingredients in a pinch

A dish made with 4 ingredients that makes for a perfect mid week meal


Ingredients:

  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs (1 per person plus 1 extra)

Steps:

  1. Separate the egg yolks and keep in a bowl. Combine with the grated Pecorino Romano
  2. Slice the guanciale into lardons and fry. Once cooked remove from the pan and keep the oil separate. Turn off the heat.
  3. Add some of the oil into the egg and cheese mixture and combine.
  4. Boil the spaghetti for 8 minutes until al dente. Drain but keep some of the pasta water.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan used for the guanciale and coat in the oil from the pan.
  6. Pour the egg mix over the top with some of the pasta waterand mix together with the spagetti
  7. Serve with the cooked guanciale on top.
