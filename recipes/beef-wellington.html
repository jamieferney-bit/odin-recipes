Beef Wellington

A delicious beef wellington

A fillet of beef encased in a mushroom duxelle, parma ham and wrapped in flaky shortcrust pastry.


Ingredients:

  • 1 Fillet of beef
  • 400g mushrooms
  • English Mustard
  • 50g Parma Ham
  • 400g Puff Pastry

Steps:

  1. Sear the fillet of beef
  2. Apply thin coat of english mustard
  3. Blend and fry mushrooms with garlic and thyme
  4. Roll out pastry and lay the ham on top in an even layer
  5. Spread the cooled mushrooms on top of the ham
  6. Place the beef on top and roll
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
